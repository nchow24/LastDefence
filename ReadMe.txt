STARTING LAST DEFENCE:

open LastDefencePlay.exe

GAMEPLAY:

Features implemented in the game demo:
- level 1
- boss level
- shop
- map (level select)
- cinematics (between scenes)

Use your tank to destory enemies and defeat level 1
	Use the left and right keypad arrows to move the tank
	Use the mouse to aim
	Use the left mouse click to fire missles 

Defeating level 1 will unlock the boss level.

Use funds gained from destroying enemies to upgrade your tank in the shop (located from the map).

Note: funds and score are the same. Thus you must optimize your upgrades to ensure your purchases are worth it!

Upgrades:
Size - size of bullet
Rate - latency of firing missile
Health - adds an extra heart for all future levels
Speed - speed of missile
